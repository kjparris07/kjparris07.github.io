Resume



Description


For this assignment, I was tasked with creating a resume — a brief written summary of one’s qualifications, academic history, and work background.



Reflection


The main challenge that I encountered in the process of creating this resume was keeping consistent verb tenses and formatting, since this basic resume has gone through a variety of changes throughout the years. I overcame this challenge by reading through and reviewing the resume with a discerning eye, as well as showing it to others for them to review.


I learned that resume writing requires active verbs and specific, measurable accomplishments. I learned that I do actually have these accomplishments, I just have to consider each job position more carefully.
