Description


For this assignment, I was tasked with creating an infographic on any topic, so long as it was clear and visually appealing. I chose to discuss ways to care for one's mental health.



Reflection


The main challenge that I encountered in this assignment was physically fitting all of the text onto the page in a legible font size, or deciding what to remove. I overcame this difficulty by carefully selecting a medium font size, then adjusting the formatting until I was satisfied. I decided to remove additonal text and paraphrase it if it still would not fit after the reformatting.


I learned that graphic design is far more difficult than people give it credit for. There is a thin line between conciseness and crowdedness that is very easy to trample. Additionally, I learned that I enjoy color and coming up with palettes.
