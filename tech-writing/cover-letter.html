Description


For this assignment, I was tasked with creating a cover letter addressed to a potential employer, proving myself as a capable candidate.



Reflection


The main challenge that I encountered in this assignment was speaking about myself confidently enough without coming across as boastful. Often, simple confidence comes across as vain to me, so I have to push through those feelings to make sure the cover letter can serve its purpose.


I learned that cover letters are very specific to each position because they require you to tailor your strengths to what the employer is specifically seeking. Also, I learned that some of my more general job experiences – like retail or service work – still demonstrate skills to employers, like strong communication or problem-solving.
