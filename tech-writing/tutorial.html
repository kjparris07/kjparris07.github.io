Tutorial Video



Description


For this assignment, my partner and I were tasked with creating a tutorial video and accompanying materials on the topic of our choice. We chose to create instructions to upload a Google Document to Canvas.




Reflection


The main challenge that I encountered in the process of creating this video was keeping the video brief while still including enough steps to make it easy to follow.


Additionally, I learned that my inclination is to go into as much detail as possible, which many situations do not call for.
